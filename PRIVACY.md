# Privacy Policy

Fluid Notes respects your privacy.

- The extension does **not collect, store, or transmit** any personal or identifiable information.
- All data (notes, preferences, themes) is stored locally on your browser using the Chrome Storage API.
- No cloud sync or third-party tracking is used.
- The extension does not require login or internet access to function.

If you have questions, please contact **alekarpy@gmail.com**.
